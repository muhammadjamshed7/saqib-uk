Flat Roof Solutions

We provide comprehensive flat roofing solutions using top-quality materials including EPDM rubber, fibreglass (GRP), felt, and modified bitumen. Our flat roof systems are designed for maximum durability and weather resistance to protect your property for years to come.

Our Services Include:

  • New flat roof installation
  • Flat roof replacement and refurbishment
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Surface coating and waterproofing
  • Insulation upgrades for energy efficiency
  • Gutter and drainage solutions for flat roofs

Our skilled team can assess your current flat roof and recommend the most appropriate solution, whether it's a complete replacement or targeted repairs. We prioritize proper drainage and vapor control to extend the lifespan of your flat roof.
